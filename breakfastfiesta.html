Optimal Delivery Routes using Python and Bing Maps API

Context

One of my closest friends is the owner of a local Miami-based business called breakfastfiesta which started seeing a lot of success around 2019. Her day-to-day operations are very manageable, but every now and then on special occasions like Valentine's Day and Mother's Day the demand can be overwhelming.

On Valentine's Day 2020 I helped her out with deliveries, and it stuck out to me that some of my first places were far away from her house in places like Kendall and Coral Gables, and although they were close to each other, they were kind of "disconnected" from the rest. We agreed that there might be a better way to plan the routes to save time and that it would definitely be worth it to look into. We finished the day as normal, and started making plans on implementing a new delivery strategy for Mother's Day. It was also around this time that I saw the traveling salesman problem in my OR class.
